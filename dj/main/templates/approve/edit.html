Directions:
Scroll down to the previews of the raw files that a db query has assigned to your talk. Given that changes were made to the schedule and that not all talks start on time it may be incorrect. Find the raw files that should be used as part of your talk. [x]Apply the ones that sould be included, remove the [x] from ones that should not. Normally this will be the default. You can trim from the start and end by entering the time in the Start and End boxes. The time you enter is the time you see on the player.
