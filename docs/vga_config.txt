PRESENTATION: GREEN/GREEN - Presenter's laptop on both monitor and projector.
BETWEEN TALKS / SETUP: RED/GREEN - Presenter's laptop on monitor, slide show on projector.

dark: off/off - nothing on monitor or slideshow
debug: red/red - slide show to both monitor and projector.

Normal operation:  
Push button 1 to flip projector between slide show and presenter's laptop.
Start with red-green.  slide show projected, monitor shows presenter's laptop.  presenter hooks up laptop and figures out how to get his display showing on monitor.
When talk is about to start: press button 1 twice. lights are now green-green.  Presenter gives talk.
When talk is over, press button 1 once.  This puts back to red-geen.

Buttons:
1 (right): projector  
2 (left): lcd monitor

Lights:
Green(A): presenter's laptop
Red(B): slide show 
Off: no output


Cable Connections:
B (top) - from slide show vga card (SLAVE)
A (bottom left) - from presenter's laptop (PRES)
2 (middle) - to lcd monitor (LCD)
1 (right) - to twinpact in (Twinpact IN)
--twinpact out to projector (venue cable)
